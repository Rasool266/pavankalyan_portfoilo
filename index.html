From Power Star to People’s Leader

Pawan Kalyan (born 2 September 1971) is a celebrated Telugu film actor, martial artist, and the founder of Jana Sena Party. He currently serves as the Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, balancing cinema, politics, and philanthropy.
